Who I Am

About Me

Jason Yaasiel is a first-year undergraduate studying Information Systems at Sabaragamuwa University of Sri Lanka. Originally from Batticaloa, he brings a strong academic foundation and a genuine passion for technology that extends well beyond the classroom.

His interests lie primarily in cybersecurity and computer networking — areas where he holds professional certifications from IVTC Campus. He is drawn to the challenge of building secure, reliable systems and understanding how digital infrastructure works at its core.

Beyond academics, Jason has demonstrated consistent leadership — serving as a school prefect and as an active member of the Lions Club of Batticaloa Supreme. He believes that community impact and technical excellence go hand in hand.
