About Me

I'm Jason Yaasiel, an Information Systems undergraduate at Sabaragamuwa University of Sri Lanka (SUSL), Academic Year 2024/25. I'm driven by a curiosity for how technology solves real-world problems — especially in cybersecurity, databases, and software systems.
